Challenger Approaching! Roy Revealed!

Posted: September 18, 2013
Share Tweet Submit

Wait, that's not our usual logo! That's because it's our new logo. And with a new logo comes other things...


We recently introduced Turbo Tuesdays, but we're not done yet with announcements! Someone new has joined the roster, prepare yourself for...




Roy, the son of Eliwood, returns to the battlefield! It's been a long time coming but the team is happy to finally reveal this character. It was a long thought dream of the team to be able to bring back an old favorite but now that dream is reality! Roy does not replace anyone at all, he has his own character select slot and icon. We've worked hard to restore Roy to his former glory and also give him some new tools. We hope you all will look forward to playing this character once again.


Be sure to head on over to his character page for a more in-depth overview of his design!


--The PMBR
