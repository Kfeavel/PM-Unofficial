New Feature In Demo 2.5: Stage Select Screen Redesign!

Posted: December 18, 2012
Share Tweet Submit




After much planning, coding, and hard work, we are excited to give you the first look at our new Stage Select Screen for Demo 2.5! We've carefully crafted this new screen to be both visually pleasing and convenient for players. In this design, the stage preview has been given more emphasis, and the stages are neatly aligned in a format intuitive to casual and competitive players.


We paid special attention to detail when designing the order of stages. Stages are now grouped together based on key components of their design. This includes platform layout, overall size, series franchise, and hazards. Also, the most basic of stages are located towards the bottom, while the more complex stages can be found on the top row. This will make stage selection more convenient during tournaments, as long stage lists will no longer need to be memorized.


Speaking of tournament convenience, we've also added stage striking to the Stage Select Screen!




You can now strike stages with the X button!


Built with tournament play in mind, the Stage Strike code works by highlighting any stage and pressing the X button. This will replace the stage icon with an "X" and prevent the stage from being selected. Press Z to restore all of the stages to normal. It's also possible to strike all stages that are not enabled in Random by pressing Y. Additionally, you can now switch pages by pressing the L or R button. Convenient? Very!




When no stage is highlighted, a classy Project M decal fills the void!
