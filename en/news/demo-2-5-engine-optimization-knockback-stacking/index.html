Demo 2.5 Engine Optimization: Knockback Stacking!

Posted: November 3, 2012
Share Tweet Submit


Thanks to one of our lead developers, Magus, Project M 2.5 now offers fully functional knockback (KB) stacking! In Melee, if a player was hit within 10 frames after an attack, the second hit's KB would completely replace the KB and hitstun of the original attack. However, if a player was hit after those 10 frames, the hitstun would still be replaced, but the KB vectors for the two moves would be added together when they are in opposing directions (and the greater of the two is used when they are not). All sorts of combos will now feel much more "Meleesque" with this mechanic change
