Project M on Nintendo Enthusiast

Posted: March 30, 2014
Share Tweet Submit



We hope everyone has been enjoying Project M 3.02 for the last few months! We've been busy working on the next release, whenever it may come, and enjoying all of the wonderful feedback and events featuring PM as of late.

Be sure to like us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter if you haven't already to keep up with everything going on in the world of Project M.

We'd also like let everyone know that Nintendo Enthusiast recently spoke to TKBreezy and our very own Strong Bad to write up a fantastic article covering Project M's history and current standings in the Smash world. Take a look here!

And if you didn't know, we've been added as an official game on Twitch! If you plan on streaming Project M on Twitch, select "Project M" and you'll show up on the Twitch directory for Project M.

Enjoy!
