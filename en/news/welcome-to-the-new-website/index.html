Welcome To The New Website

Posted: June 26, 2013
Share Tweet Submit

Hello and welcome to the new home for all things Project M! As you can see, we've gotten quite a bit of a face lift, and even have a proper domain now! This new design is courtesy of one of our latest introductions to the PMBR, Warchamp7.


We hope you guys like the new look and find it more intuitive to find any information you're looking for. Be sure to check out the new Stages page, and keep an eye out for us to finish up the Features page, which will eventually detail all of the cool new changes we've made to Project M.


We'll of course be adding anything we feel necessary to the site, as this update also makes it easier for us to modify the content and make changes wherever needed. This means you should look forward to hearing more from us in the future!




I'm sure many of you are happy to hear this, but are looking for a little bit more. Look no further! We are officially announcing Project M 2.6 right now, to be released before the end of July. We'd love to give you a more concrete date, and we will do so as soon as we have one.


Stay tuned for more details on 2.6 in the coming days, and be sure to check the website from time to time, you never know what you might find...
