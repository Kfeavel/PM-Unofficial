Demo 2.5 Roster Reveal: Zero Suit Samus!

Posted: December 15, 2012
Share Tweet Submit




The speedy, stealthy, and sexy Zero Suit Samus sneaks in and takes on the rest of the Project M cast for Demo 2.5! Without her power suit, Samus sports very high mobility, giving her a number of options in and out of any situation and an impressive combo-game.
She is very adept at creating openings and punishing mistakes - keep your guard up!




Specials
Neutral Special: Paralyzer
Side Special: Plasma Whip
Up Special: Plasma Wire
Down Special: Flip Jump


Don't forget to check out Zero Suit Samus' Character Page for more in-depth information on the character. Stay tuned for more updates on Demo 2.5!
