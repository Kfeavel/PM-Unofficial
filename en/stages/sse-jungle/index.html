Stages

SSE Jungle

Stage Size: Very Large
Platforms: 4
Ceiling: Low
Side Blast Zones: Close


It would be a stretch to call this Olimar's home stage. Instead of trying to rehabilitate the whimsical death trap that is Distant Planet, we decided to make a completely new design from the Brawl's SubSpace Emmisary. The level is based on the jungle outside the Bomb Facility where we first meet our space traveling hero and his gang of two-eyed vegetables.


An abnormally long stage, but fun nonetheless, SSE Jungle sports an unorthodox platform distribution, allowing for some creative combos and edgeguards. Although 'stage control' is a very loose term on this stage, the low ceiling and close blast zones are conducive to early kills.
