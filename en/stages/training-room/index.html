Training Room

Stage Size: Very Large
Platforms: 0
Ceiling: Very High
Side: Close


In many competitive fighting games, the training mode stage is one of the most important tools for an improving player to use. There is a lot to be aware of when learning one's way through a Smash title, and so this Training Stage features visual and kinetic tools that encourage experimentation and make it easier than ever for players to hone their craft.


This stage features a large wall on the left side of the stage helpful for testing knockback angles and wallbounce effects, as well as a single ledge on the the opposite, allowing players to simulate the intricate gameplay that the ledge provides. But where this stage stands out, is in the units that glow in the background separating the space between characters. You can utilize these units for many purposes, from seeing how long a particular attack is... to testing the distance of your character's glide toss. Anything is possible with hard work and dedication!


To answer a frequently asked question about this stage, this does replace the Wifi Waiting Room, which should add for some interesting practice time before a Wifi match!
