Stages

Mario Circuit

Stage Size: Medium
Platforms: 3
Ceiling: Medium
Side Blast Zones: Small


Ready...set... GO! Welcome to Mario Circuit, the only race track to be featured in every Mario Kart game! To hold that distinction, it has gone through a variety of makeovers, but we are happy to see that the Circuit is back to good old Figure-8 form. It makes sense that it'd go back to classic style; who could compete with the crazy tracks of F-Zero! Whatever the reason, we're happy that it's found a niche in the Project M stage lineup.


As I always say, the best place to fight is in the middle of the street where someone's racing. Sure, they may be Shy Guys, but they won't be shy when hitting you! Unlike the series from which this stage hails, the person behind won't be receiving a Bullet Bill or Golden Mushroom. They're going to have to use the two halves of this stage to their advantage. The top half receives little warning in regards to the racers, but has an easier time of KO'ing both vertically and horizontally. The bottom half gets plenty of time to react to the karts and can use that to force their opponents to a side to kill horizontally, but vertical kills require high percentages. It's probably in your best interest to not play King of the Hill on this stage, that middle platform is quite the danger zone.
